Nicolas Dias is a Computer Science student at the Pontifical Catholic University of Minas Gerais (PUC Minas). Rather than taking on a traditional corporate role during his studies, Nicolas dedicates his time to building complex software and making impactful open-source contributions, most notably to the Rizin framework. Currently, his primary focus outside of Rizin is rawnn—an advanced machine learning library he is developing entirely in C. Originally inspired by the book Neural Networks from Scratch in Python, Nicolas has heavily expanded upon its foundation, transforming rawnn into an actively developed library with significantly more features and capabilities. Looking ahead, Nicolas aims to leverage his strong background in systems programming and open-source development to pursue a career at Cadence Design Systems.
